# Cake Album ERP
**Enterprise Bakery Management System**
Cake Album Operations System – An intelligent workflow platform that manages every stage of cake production from customer order to delivery and payment confirmation.
Cake Album Operations System is a comprehensive bakery management platform developed to digitize and automate the entire Cake Album production workflow. The system manages customer orders, production planning, baking, decoration, quality control, packaging, delivery, inventory, finance, and executive analytics from a single application.


#Cake Album Operations System

A comprehensive bakery Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) platform designed to digitize and automate the complete bakery workflow—from customer order placement to production, inventory management, delivery, finance, customer follow-up, and executive reporting.

---

## Overview

Cake Album Operations System is an all-in-one business management platform developed specifically for **Cake Album Uganda**. The system replaces manual processes with a centralized digital workflow that enables every department to collaborate in real time while giving management complete visibility into business operations.

The platform manages the entire customer journey, including:

- Customer Orders
- Production Planning
- Baking Operations
- Filling & Piling
- Covering
- Decoration
- Design & Innovation (Topper Production)
- Quality Control
- Packaging
- Delivery & Dispatch
- Finance
- Procurement & Materials Tracking
- Customer Follow-up & Complaints
- Staff Management
- Executive Analytics

The goal is simple:

> **Deliver every customer order on time, maintain exceptional quality, improve accountability, and provide management with real-time operational insights.**
